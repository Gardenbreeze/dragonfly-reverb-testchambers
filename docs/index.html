Dragonfly Reverb

Dragonfly Reverb is a bundle of two free audio effect plugins. One designed as a concert hall reverb, the other as a room reverb. These two plugins are suitable for a variety of musical styles.

If you are upgrading from 1.0.x or 1.1.x, please delete the old plugin and replace it. Your existing projects will load the new Dragonfly Hall Reverb with backward compatible settings.
